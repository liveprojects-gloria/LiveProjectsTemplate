Onboarding

  • Alice sees the name section and types in a three letter name
  • She then selects channel red
  • She then presses enter game
  • This takes her to casual mode

Casual

  • Alice has the ability to use weapons, as there is another player already in the game
  • Alice wants to race the other player
  • Alice presses the challenge button to send a request to the other player

Challenge

  • Alice has sent the request and is now waiting for a response from her opponent Bob.

Challenge Request

  • Bob has received a request from Alice
  • Bob Also wants to race
  • Bob Presses Accept

Race

  • Alice regains her controls
  • The race with bob has begun
  • She has just received a weapon
  • She decides to deploy the weapon
  • She presses oil slick

Oil Slick

  • Bobs Car spins out of control
  • Alice receives a message to confirming the hit
  • The weapons box becomes blocked
  • Alice is no longer able to use a weapon for 15 seconds

Shield

  • After 15 seconds Alice obtains another weapon
  • She is notified that she has a shield
  • Her shield box becomes ticked

Deflected

  • Alice is attacked with a smart bomb
  • As she has a shield it is deflected
  • The shield has been used and unticked
  • Alice then completes the rest of the race

position

  • Alice's controls are disabled
  • Alice is told to wait
  • Her car is automatically powered until it reaches the sensor/start line

Countdown

  • A traffic light overlay appears on Alice's screen
  • It cycles through the colours: red, amber, green
  • When the light goes green Alice is taken to race mode

Win

  • Alice completed all 15 laps before Bob
  • She is congratulated
  • She is presented with her fastest lap time
  • She compares it to the record lap
  • She then exits back to casual mode
